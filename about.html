In Current Railway Reservation System Project User Faces various difficulties while booking
their tickets by visiting to the reservation counter or by visiting to the agents.
Train Ticket Booking System Project will save customers time and money as well.

To access this Train Ticket Booking System Project, users have to register
by giving their entire personal details like Name,DOB,Gender,Mobile No, Email Id etc...
After successful registration,users will be provided with their login id and password.


Finding trains between given routes through simple search query on
particular date and displaying all details of that particular train such as
arrival time, departure time,number of seats available,class type,
charges details and many more.


Current Train Reservation System does not provide other facilities such as
shortest route details from their current locations.
It does not provide details of their previous booking history as well as festive
session offer/discounts details.
